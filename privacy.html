Privacy Policy for SimpleTerms

Last Updated: December 30, 2024

Introduction

SimpleTerms ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Privacy Policy Content: When you use our extension to analyze a privacy policy, we temporarily process the text content of that policy.
  • Usage Statistics: We store locally (on your device) basic usage information including the number of analyses performed and installation date.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or activity on other websites
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Financial or payment information
  • Personal communications or messages

How We Use Information

Privacy Policy Analysis

  • Content is sent to our secure Google Cloud Function for AI-powered analysis
  • Analysis provides risk scores and plain-language summaries
  • Content is processed in real-time and immediately discarded
  • No permanent storage of your privacy policy content

Service Improvement

  • Anonymous usage statistics help us understand extension performance
  • Data is stored locally on your device only
  • Used to implement freemium features (analysis limits)

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Google Cloud Platform

  • Privacy policy text is sent to Google Cloud Function for AI analysis
  • Google processes this data according to their AI/ML Privacy Policy
  • Processing is temporary and content is not stored by Google

Google AI (Gemini)

  • Our Cloud Function uses Google's Gemini AI for text analysis
  • Subject to Google Cloud Privacy Policy
  • Content is processed but not used for AI model training

No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for any purpose.

Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Temporary Processing: Content is only held in memory during analysis
  • Local Storage: Usage statistics are stored locally on your device
  • No Persistent Storage: We do not maintain databases of user content

Your Rights and Choices

Extension Controls

  • You can uninstall the extension at any time
  • Local usage data is automatically removed upon uninstallation
  • You control which privacy policies to analyze

Data Minimization

  • We only process data necessary for analysis functionality
  • No tracking across websites or persistent user profiling
  • Content is only temporarily processed for analysis and immediately discarded

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • Extension update notifications
  • Updates to this page
  • GitHub repository announcements

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/C-Chambers/SimpleTerms
  • Issues: Open an issue on our GitHub repository

Compliance

This extension is designed to comply with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General privacy best practices
  • Minimal data collection principles

This privacy policy is effective as of December 30, 2024.
